Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you join our affiliate program or interact with our platform, we collect:

  • Your name, email, phone, and business information
  • Professional credentials (license numbers, certifications)
  • Business details (practice type, patient volume)
  • Bank and payment information for commission processing
  • Referral and patient interaction data (anonymized)
  • Website analytics and engagement metrics
  • Communication records and support interactions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Manage your affiliate account and program participation
  • Process and pay commissions accurately
  • Track referrals and performance metrics
  • Send program updates and marketing materials
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Conduct analytics to improve our program
  • Comply with healthcare and regulatory requirements

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share data with:

  • MaxLifeMD and its healthcare partners for program operations
  • Payment processors and financial institutions for commissions
  • Service providers (analytics, hosting, customer support)
  • Regulatory agencies and healthcare authorities when required
  • Professional advisors for compliance matters

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our platform uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, remember preferences, and track program performance. These help us understand how you use our affiliate portal and optimize functionality. You can manage cookie settings in your browser preferences.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Under New Jersey privacy laws and federal regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your information where permitted
  • Opt out of promotional communications
  • Receive your data in portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@joinmaxlifemd.com or 732-484-3000.

6. Healthcare Privacy and HIPAA Compliance

As we handle health-related referrals, we maintain strict confidentiality of any patient information. We comply with healthcare privacy regulations and do not disclose protected health information without proper authorization. All referral data is processed securely.

7. Data Security

We implement encryption, secure servers, firewalls, and restricted access controls to protect your information. We follow industry best practices for data protection. However, no system is completely secure. We encourage strong passwords and immediate reporting of any suspicious activity.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our affiliate program is for healthcare professionals and business owners, not children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover that a child has submitted information, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians may request removal by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our practices evolve. Changes will be posted with an updated effective date, and your continued participation indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us
