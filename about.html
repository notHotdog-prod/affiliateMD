Our Story

Built for Partners Who Care

MaxLifeMD was founded by Bryan and Kirk, two entrepreneurs with a simple observation: the best healthcare professionals — chiropractors, personal trainers, gym owners, wellness coaches — often struggle to earn additional revenue from their patient relationships.

We saw practitioners leaving money on the table because they lacked a reliable, compliant way to refer patients to telemedicine HRT and peptide therapy services.

So we built MaxLifeMD. A partnership program designed specifically for wellness professionals. High commissions ($25-$50/patient/month recurring). Zero medical liability. Physician-supervised care. And a platform that makes it incredibly easy to integrate into your practice.
